About FE Academy

FE Academy, short for Firdaus Eisner Academy, is an online academy established with the purpose of teaching programming by two of its founders, Firdaus and Khairi. As the academy grew, we expanded our offerings to include a business syllabus with the help of our third founder, Asyraf.

We originally started on Patreon, where our learning plans were first introduced. Today, we continue to offer our programmes through Patreon while also sharing our plans and updates via social media and this website.

Why “Eisner”? It is a reference to Byleth Eisner, the Fire Emblem hero who is also a teacher — a small nod to the idea of guidance, growth, and learning together. ^_^
